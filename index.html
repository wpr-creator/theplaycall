Assignment Overview
One Event. Two Writing Styles.

In The Play Call, you will study one real moment when the U.S. government used fiscal policy to intervene in the economy. You will write about the same event in two styles: one creative piece and one analytical piece. Each piece will be four paragraphs and should explain how the policy worked, who benefited, and who paid the cost.

Required Term Bank
Use at least two of these terms in each writing style.
Fiscal Policy
Government use of taxing and spending to influence the economy
Expansionary Fiscal Policy
Government action to stimulate a slowing economy by increasing spending or cutting taxes
Contractionary Fiscal Policy
Government action to slow an overheating economy by cutting spending or raising taxes
Multiplier Effect
Initial government spending creates a larger increase in total economic activity
Progressive Tax
Higher earners pay a larger percentage of their income
Regressive Tax
Lower earners pay a higher share of income in practice
Budget Deficit
Government spends more in a year than it collects in tax revenue
National Debt
Total accumulated amount the federal government owes from deficit spending
