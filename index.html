The Play Call

Every time the government intervenes in the economy, it makes a decision. Some effects are visible right away. Others take longer to see. Your job is to understand both.

Assignment Overview
One Event. Two Writing Styles.

In The Play Call, you will analyze one real moment when the U.S. government used fiscal policy to respond to a problem in the economy. You will write about the same event in one creative style and one analytical style. Each response must be four paragraphs.
